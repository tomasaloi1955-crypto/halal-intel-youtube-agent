# Privacy Policy — Halal Intelligence

_Last updated: 2026-07-06_

Halal Intelligence ("the App") is a personal automation tool that publishes short
technology videos to the owner's own social media channels (YouTube, TikTok).

## What data we use
- The App uses OAuth access tokens that **you** authorize, solely to upload videos
  to **your own** accounts.
- The App does **not** collect, store, sell, or share personal data of any third party.
- The App does **not** access your followers, messages, or private information.

## Tokens & security
- Access/refresh tokens are stored as encrypted secrets and are used only to publish
  content you created. You can revoke access at any time in your TikTok/Google account
  security settings.
